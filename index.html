About me

My research focuses on developing ultrasound and photoacoustic imaging systems to explore the brain and skin. I work primarily on the experimental side, where I design and conduct experiments using rodents and larger animals like sheep. The goal is to evaluate and fine-tune these imaging systems, optimizing them for better image quality and more accurate tissue analysis.

In addition, I am involved in signal and image post-processing to further enhance clarity and improve visualization of tissue properties—ensuring these techniques remain non-invasive while providing detailed insights.
